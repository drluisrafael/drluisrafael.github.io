About Dr. Luis Rafael Martinez

Dr. Luis Rafael Martinez, MD, is an orthopedic surgeon specializing in orthopedic surgery, spine surgery, and trauma care.

His approach focuses on accurate diagnosis, evidence-based treatment, modern surgical techniques, and personalized care designed around each patient's needs.

With a commitment to restoring mobility and improving quality of life, Dr. Martinez combines medical expertise with compassionate patient communication throughout every stage of treatment.

Areas of Focus

Orthopedic Surgery

Treatment of injuries and conditions affecting bones, joints, muscles, and soft tissues.

Spine Surgery

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of spinal disorders.

Trauma Surgery

Management of complex orthopedic injuries and fractures.
